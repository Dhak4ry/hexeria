About us

1.Who are we?

We are a group of friends who want to show you a project about Water / Air Transport and Pollution

2.What are our goals?

We want to show the people what causes the Water / Air Pollution and how to prevent it.

3.What do we want to achieve?

The main idea is to stop people polluting the oceans, rivers and other types of water sources. Also we want to stop the air pollution from gas emissions, oil refining and more.
